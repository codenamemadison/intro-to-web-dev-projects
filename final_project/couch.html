The Item

Item Description:

My friend's gray couch

Location & Means of Recival:

My friend's apartment in Brooklyn

Special Details:

Ideal nap location

Time Period of Impact:

Fall 2020 - Spring 2021

The Story

During quarantine, our friend Long's apartment was the go-to place for me and a few other friends to go to. Whether it was to study together because we were taking the same classes, talk for hours and hours about our lives, get food and coming back to eat, accidentally fall asleep, or be the default place to go to after recitation, the apartment was our hangout spot for spending time together during a time where we couldn't really go out. Although this object compared to the others is not one that I own myself, it is associated a lot with these memories since its where they all took place. Serving as the only place to sit as a guest, the couch has accidentally made its place in our hang-out environment at the time.

On the left, for example, it was where we sat and hang out during our "Galentine's celebration".
